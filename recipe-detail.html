Spicy Beef Curry

This recipe brings the bold flavors of Indian spices combined with tender beef, simmered to perfection.

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables and meat into bite-sized pieces. Make sure the beef is cut against the grain for maximum tenderness.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add your spices and toast them for 30 seconds until fragrant, then add the beef and brown on all sides.
  3. Add the onions, garlic, ginger, and tomatoes. Stir well to combine all ingredients and let simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beef is tender and the sauce has thickened.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (chuck or stewing beef), cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 3 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated

Tools

  • Sharp knife for chopping
  • Large cooking pot or Dutch oven
  • Wooden stirring spoon
  • Measuring spoons
